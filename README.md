# TrueBlue

TrueBlue (NYSE: TBI) is a leading provider of workforce solutions and specialized talent services. TrueBlue connects people and work through three operating segments: PeopleReady (industrial staffing), PeopleScout (RPO and talent advisory), and Staff Management | SMX (managed staffing). TrueBlue's digital platforms include JobStack for on-demand industrial staffing and Affinix for recruitment process outsourcing.

**URL:** [apis.yml](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/api-evangelist/trueblue/refs/heads/main/apis.yml)

## APIs

TrueBlue does not currently expose a public developer API. Their technology platforms (JobStack, Affinix, Stafftrack) are proprietary and accessible only through TrueBlue's client portals.
